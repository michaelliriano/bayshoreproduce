What We Do

At Bayshore Produce, our mission is to provide our customers with the best quality produce at the most competitive price. Bayshore Produce was founded by Rosann Cabrera in 2010 after working in the industry for 17 years as a buyer/broker. Now with over 25 years of produce experience and an experienced staff, our goal is still the same; to help our customers be successful.
